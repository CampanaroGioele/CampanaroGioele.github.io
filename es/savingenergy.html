Saving energy at home

  • Reduce the production of waste and reuse.
  • Recycle as much as possible: cans, paper, glass and plastic.
  • Save electricity: use only what you need.
  • Save petrol: walk, ride your bike, take a bus.
  • Keep doors and windows closed to avoid heat loss.
  • Use only the amount of hot water you need.
  • Saving energy helps protecting the environment.
